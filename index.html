Crystal Quest!

Collect just the right amount to win!

Charge up Magic Crystals by combining less powerful ones.

There are four crystals below. By clicking a crystal you will add a specific amount of magic power to your Magic Crystal.

You win the game by matching your crystal power to the Magic Crystal you are charging, if you exceed the power of the Crystal it will burn out!

The power of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it.

Each time the game starts the game will change the power in each crystal.
